Florentine Finish

CFF - Caroline Florentine Finish

A signature technique of Carolina Bucci first
mastered in the family workshops in the 19th
century, the Florentine Finish is a precise, time-consuming treatment with a beautiful reward. Every part of the gold is hand-beaten with a diamond-
tipped tool, leaving faceted indentations on the
surface and subtle sparkle that lasts.
